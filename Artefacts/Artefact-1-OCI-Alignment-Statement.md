## OCI Alignment Statement

GapMap MATE is a product within the Operational Capability Intelligence (OCI) ecosystem.

GapMap MATE supports the OCI mission:

> Make workforce capability visible, measurable, and actionable.

and the OCI purpose:

> Help people understand what they can do, what they need next, and how to move forward.

All product decisions should support one or more of the OCI pillars:

- **Situational Awareness**
- **Operational Readiness**
- **Workforce Capability**

GapMap MATE contributes to OCI by helping serving personnel and service leavers gain personal operational awareness of their capabilities, opportunities, readiness, and next steps.

---

## Product Mission

Help serving personnel and service leavers understand what they can do, what they need next, and how to move forward with confidence.

---

## Product Purpose

Translate military experience into civilian opportunities through structured guidance, capability understanding, and practical next steps.

---

## Product Promise

When a user finishes using GapMap MATE they should be able to say:

> "I know what I can do next."

## Narrative Anchors

These are strategic reminders used to guide product direction, communications, and future development.

### Helping service leavers understand what comes next.
The primary positioning statement for GapMap MATE.

### Your resettlement starts on day one of service.
Transition is not a six-month activity before discharge.

Skills, experience, qualifications, and capability are accumulated throughout a career. GapMap MATE helps people understand and apply those capabilities throughout their journey.

### Understand what you can do. Understand what comes next.
The bridge between capability understanding and practical action.

### Personal Operational Awareness for Transition.
GapMap MATE provides individuals with the situational awareness needed to make informed transition decisions.
