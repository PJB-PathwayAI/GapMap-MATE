### Mission Status: COMPLETE

Operation PROOF was initiated to answer a single operational question:

> *Can an AI companion be engineered to support Armed Forces service leavers in a trustworthy, explainable and human-centred way?*

Following the completion of:

- Exercise LENS
- Exercise PRISM
- Exercise COMPASS

the answer is assessed as:

**Yes.**

Operation PROOF is therefore declared **Mission Complete**.

---

### Transition of Remit

Future development of GapMap MATE will transition from architectural discovery into:

1. **Operational Refinement** — polishing the companion behaviour, engine logic, and user experience based on real-world interaction
2. **Pilot Deployment** — exposing MATE to real service leavers outside the controlled exercise environment
3. **Evidence Gathering** — collecting behavioural data from genuine transitions to validate, refine, and improve

### Standing Doctrine

The methodology developed during Operation PROOF will remain the engineering doctrine for future products within the PathwayAI ecosystem.

This includes:

- The three-layer repository architecture (Doctrine → Design Intent → Engineering)
- The exercise loop: Doctrine → Design Intent → Engineering → Exercise → AAR → Behaviour Refinement → Doctrine
- The Five Behavioural Principles
- The Evidence Rule
- The Smudge Test™
- The Soak Period philosophy
- The Commander's Intent framework
