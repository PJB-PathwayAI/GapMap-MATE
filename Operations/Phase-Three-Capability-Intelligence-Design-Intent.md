# OPERATION PROOF
# PHASE THREE — CAPABILITY INTELLIGENCE
# DESIGN INTENT v1.0

**Status:** FOR ENGINEERING
**Date:** 5 July 2026
**From:** Founder
**To:** Chief Engineer

---

## 1. Mission

The Capability Intelligence Engine exists to translate a confirmed Operational Picture into an evidence-backed Capability Picture.

It does not recommend careers.
It does not recommend learning.
It does not generate action plans.

Its sole responsibility is helping the individual understand the capabilities they already possess.

## 2. Principle

**Reveal capability. Never invent it.**

Capabilities are discovered through evidence.
They are never assumed.
Every capability must be explainable.
Every capability must be traceable.

## 3. Purpose

The purpose of Phase Three is to answer one question:

**"What am I demonstrably capable of?"**

Not: "What job should I do?"

That comes later.

## 4. Inputs

The engine may only operate once:
- Operational Picture confirmed
- Assessment Confidence sufficient
- UserProfile validated
- Evidence Log available

If these conditions are not met: Do not evaluate capability.

## 5. Outputs

The Capability Intelligence Engine produces a **Capability Picture**:

- Evidence-backed capabilities
- Each capability contains:
  - Capability Name
  - Civilian Translation
  - Evidence References
  - Confidence Score
  - Transferability Notes

No recommendations. No career suggestions. No learning pathways.

## 6. Evidence Rule

Every capability must be supported by evidence.

Evidence may originate from:
- Conversation
- Service History
- Qualifications
- CV
- User Statements
- Assessments

Each capability references the Evidence Log.

If evidence cannot be identified: The capability cannot be created.

## 7. Confidence

Capability Confidence is separate from Assessment Confidence.

- Assessment Confidence asks: "How well do we understand the person?"
- Capability Confidence asks: "How confident are we that this capability is evidenced?"

These measurements are independent.

## 8. Translation

The engine translates military experience into civilian capability.

Example:
- Military: Section Commander
- Capability: Leadership, Delegation, Planning, Decision Making, Mentoring, Operational Coordination

The military role is evidence. The capability is the output.

## 9. Capability Picture

The Capability Picture should feel like discovery.
The individual should recognise themselves.
The objective is not surprise — the objective is clarity.

The user should finish this phase thinking:
**"I hadn't realised I was already doing those things."**

## 10. Companion Behaviour

Smudge presents capability as observation. Not judgement.

Example:
> "From everything we've explored together, one capability keeps appearing."

Not:
> "You are definitely a leader."

Smudge should invite recognition. Never impose identity.

## 11. Explainability

Every capability must answer: **Why do I believe this?**

Evidence should be visible.
The individual should understand exactly how each capability was identified.
Nothing should appear as AI magic.

## 12. Non-Goals

This phase does not:
- Match jobs
- Recommend learning
- Generate CVs
- Build action plans
- Prioritise pathways
- Compare salaries

Those belong to later phases.

## 13. Success Criteria

Phase Three is complete when:
- Every capability is evidence-backed
- Every capability has provenance
- Confidence is calculated
- The Capability Picture is generated
- The individual recognises themselves in the output
- No unsupported assumptions exist

## 14. Exercise LENS

**Validation Exercise**

Mission: Can the Capability Intelligence Engine reveal capabilities the individual already possesses but has never consciously recognised?

Success Indicator: The individual responds with something similar to "I'd never thought about it like that."

Failure Indicators:
- Capabilities feel generic
- Capabilities cannot be explained
- Capabilities cannot be traced to evidence
- The Capability Picture feels AI-generated rather than personally discovered

## 15. Engineering Principles

- Evidence before inference
- Capability before recommendation
- Translation before suggestion
- Explain every conclusion
- Preserve user trust
- Never overstate capability
- Confidence reflects evidence, not optimism

## End State

The Operational Picture answers: **Who am I?**
The Capability Picture answers: **What can I demonstrably do?**

Only when both are complete may Phase Four begin.
