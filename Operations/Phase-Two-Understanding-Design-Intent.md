## Purpose

The purpose of Phase Two is not to collect information.

The purpose of Phase Two is to establish a shared **Operational Picture** between Smudge and the individual.

The Operational Picture becomes the foundation upon which every subsequent recommendation, capability assessment and learning pathway is built.

No recommendations should be generated until both the user and Smudge agree that the Operational Picture accurately reflects the individual's current situation.

---

## Mission

Engineer the Understanding Engine so that it builds an evidence-based Operational Picture through conversation rather than interrogation.

The user should feel understood, not processed.

---

## Commander's Intent

By the completion of Phase Two:

1. Smudge understands the individual.
2. The individual understands themselves more clearly.
3. Both share the same Operational Picture.
4. Assessment confidence is sufficient to proceed.
5. The user confirms: **"Yes, that's me."**

Only then does Phase Three begin.

---

## The Operational Picture

The Operational Picture should answer six operational questions.

### 1. Who are you?

Professional identity. Not simply name, rank, branch — but how does the individual understand themselves?

### 2. What have you done?

Operational experience. Leadership. Responsibility. Decision-making. Achievements. Real evidence. Not job titles.

### 3. Where are you now?

Current operating environment. Examples: Still serving, transition date, employment, study, family commitments, current circumstances.

### 4. Where are you trying to go?

Purpose. Ambition. Interests. Success. Not simply "What job?" — but "What future are you trying to build?"

### 5. What influences your journey?

Operational context. Constraints. Available time. Learning. Funding. Confidence. Support network. Anything that affects future decision-making.

### 6. How well do we understand the picture?

Two independent assessments:

**User Confidence** — How the individual currently feels about their transition.

**Assessment Confidence** — How confident Smudge is that sufficient evidence exists to support future recommendations.

> Assessment Confidence measures evidence quality. It must never be interpreted as a judgement of honesty or integrity.

Where confidence is low: Smudge asks. Smudge explores. Smudge never assumes.

---

## Operational Memory

Operational Memory begins during Phase Two.

The OCI Companion Service should establish:

| Category | Contains |
|---|---|
| **Stable Memory** | Information unlikely to change (service history, qualifications, identity) |
| **Evolving Memory** | Information expected to develop over time (goals, confidence, context) |
| **Session Memory** | Current operational context, outstanding questions, recent discussion |

Memory exists to preserve understanding. Not conversation history.

---

## Behavioural Principles

**Smudge should:**
- ✔ Ask naturally
- ✔ Listen carefully
- ✔ Reflect understanding
- ✔ Confirm accuracy
- ✔ Build trust

**Smudge should never:**
- ✘ Rush
- ✘ Assume
- ✘ Diagnose
- ✘ Recommend before understanding

## Engineering Acceptance Test

Phase Two is complete when the system can consistently produce an Operational Picture that both Smudge and the user agree accurately reflects reality.

**Success is not measured by completed fields.**
**Success is measured by shared understanding.**
