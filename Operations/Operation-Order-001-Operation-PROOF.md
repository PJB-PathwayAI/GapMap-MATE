# Operation Order 001 — Operation PROOF

> **Classification:** Internal — Operational Planning
> **From:** Founder | **To:** Chief Engineer
> **Status:** ACTIVE — Phase One (Mobilisation) commencing

---

## 1. Situation

Operation Doctrine has concluded successfully.

The GapMap MATE doctrine is now complete, consisting of ten foundational artefacts that define the mission, operational philosophy, partnership model, engineering principles and governance framework of the programme.

The doctrine has established what GapMap MATE must become.

**Operation PROOF now begins.**

Its purpose is to determine whether the doctrine can be successfully translated into operational capability.

Engineering is no longer tasked with creating a product.

> Engineering is tasked with proving the doctrine.

---

## 2. Mission

The engineering team will progressively implement the GapMap MATE doctrine through evidence-based software development.

Every implementation should demonstrate, validate or refine one or more principles established during Operation Doctrine.

Success will not be measured by the quantity of functionality delivered.

> Success will be measured by how faithfully the implementation reflects the doctrine and improves the experience of the individual.

---

## 3. Commander's Intent

The intent of Operation PROOF is to build an operational companion that demonstrates genuine understanding before guidance, capability before recommendation and partnership before automation.

Technology exists to serve operational purpose.

The implementation should remain modular, resilient and capable of evolving as operational learning emerges.

> Doctrine provides direction. Engineering provides proof. Evidence determines improvement.

---

## 4. Concept of Operations

Operation PROOF will be conducted through **six sequential phases**.

### Phase One — Mobilisation

**Objective:** Prepare the engineering environment and establish the delivery architecture.

**Outcomes:**
- Engineering responsibilities confirmed.
- Base44 delivery model established.
- OCI Companion Service architecture agreed.
- API strategy documented.
- Build rhythm established.

**Success Criteria:** Engineering understands the operational intent and the responsibilities of each system component.

### Phase Two — Understanding

**Objective:** Demonstrate that Smudge can build a meaningful understanding of the individual before providing advice.

**Outcomes:**
- Discovery conversations.
- Military profile.
- Service history.
- Goals.
- Personal context.
- Initial capability profile.

**Success Criteria:** Smudge can accurately reflect the individual's current situation before recommending any future actions.

### Phase Three — Capability Intelligence

**Objective:** Translate understanding into operational capability intelligence.

**Outcomes:**
- Capability Engine.
- Evidence Engine.
- Confidence assessment.
- Skills translation.
- Capability mapping.
- Readiness profile.

**Success Criteria:** Every capability assessment is supported by transparent evidence and explainable reasoning.

### Phase Four — Decision Support

**Objective:** Provide evidence-based recommendations that remain aligned to operational doctrine.

**Outcomes:**
- Learning pathways.
- Career recommendations.
- Qualification guidance.
- Funding opportunities.
- Action plans.
- Role matching.

**Success Criteria:** Recommendations remain explainable, evidence-based and preserve user choice.

### Phase Five — Transition Partnership

**Objective:** Establish Smudge as a trusted long-term transition companion.

**Outcomes:**
- Memory.
- Progress tracking.
- Milestones.
- Check-ins.
- Ongoing partnership.
- Transition lifecycle support.

**Success Criteria:** Users experience continuity, trust and growing situational awareness throughout their transition.

### Phase Six — Operational Validation

**Objective:** Validate the doctrine against operational reality.

**Outcomes:**
- User testing.
- SME feedback.
- Veteran validation.
- Engineering lessons.
- Doctrine review.
- Continuous improvement.

**Success Criteria:** Operational evidence confirms or refines the doctrine, ensuring it remains effective and relevant.

---

## 5. Responsibilities

| Role | Responsible For |
|------|-----------------|
| **Founder** | Mission, vision, commercial direction, doctrine stewardship, strategic priorities |
| **Chief Engineer** | Engineering architecture, system integration, implementation quality, technical resilience, operational capability delivery |
| **OCI Companion Service** | Operational reasoning, memory, understanding, decision orchestration, specialist engine coordination |
| **Smudge** | Human partnership, conversation, guidance, trust, transition support |

---

## 6. Engineering Principles

Operation PROOF will continue to operate under the **Engineering Charter** (Artefact 10) established during Operation Doctrine.

Key principles include:

- Intent before technology.
- Evidence before assumption.
- Shared responsibility for the truth.
- Continuous learning.
- Protect the person.
- Protect the purpose.
- Protect the trust.

---

## 7. Battle Rhythm

Operation PROOF will adopt a continuous delivery cycle:

```
Mission Planning
      ↓
Implementation
      ↓
Review
      ↓
Challenge
      ↓
Refinement
      ↓
Operational Evidence
      ↓
Repeat
```

Engineering is encouraged to surface observations where implementation challenges doctrine.

> Doctrine should never become dogma.

---

## 8. End State

Operation PROOF concludes when GapMap MATE consistently demonstrates the principles established during Operation Doctrine through working operational capability.

Success is evidenced by:

- Individuals feeling understood.
- Recommendations supported by evidence.
- Trust strengthened through transparency.
- Engineering validating the doctrine through implementation.
- Continuous improvement informed by operational learning.
